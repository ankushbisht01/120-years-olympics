The Olympics Knowledge Graph

Welcome to the Olympics Knowledge Graph!

This graph contains structured information about the Olympics from 1896 to 2020 .

In particular, it contains information about:

  • Athlete Name who participated in the olympics .
  • Height , Weight and Age of the athlete to the corresponding year of participation .
  • The sport in which that athlete participated .
  • The type of medal won by the athlete .
  • The NOC name and the name of the country they are particiaping in the olympics .
  • The season in which the olympics occured along with the name of the host city .

The Data Currently available in the Official Olympics site is not used by developers and other users in thier projects .
